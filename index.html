Medium-level task: Search --> Explore (the audience is unsure of what exactly to look for in the visualization, and as such, they are unsure of where to look on the visualization spatially speaking; in other words, the audience is left to interpret the visualization completely on their own)

Low-level task: Query --> Compare (the visualization illustrates daily maximum temperatures for a specific time frame (1979-2021), so not all targets are shown)
